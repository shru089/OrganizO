## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

🌿 **OrganizO — Structure your life, gently.**

Tired of productivity apps that feel stressful? OrganizO is the calm, focused workspace designed to help you organize your life without overwhelm.

With a beautiful glassmorphic interface inspired by bamboo forests, OrganizO turns your daily planning into a peaceful ritual, not a chore.

---

**✨ WHAT YOU GET (100% Free, No Sign-Up)**

📋 **Tasks** — Capture everything that matters. Add, complete, and organize your day with simple, beautiful to-do lists with priority tags.

✨ **Habits** — Build lasting habits effortlessly. Daily tracking, streak counters, and progress bars keep you on track without pressure.

⏱️ **Focus Timer** — Pomodoro-style sessions to help you enter deep work. Switch between Focus, Short Break, and Long Break modes.

📅 **Calendar** — Full monthly view with event tracking. Plan your days with clarity and see your schedule at a glance.

📊 **Dashboard** — Your personalized command center. See daily greetings, intentions, habits, and tasks all in one peaceful view.

💡 **Insights** — Understand your productivity patterns. Track your focus sessions, completed tasks, and activity over time.

🎯 **Daily Intentions** — Set your single most important goal for the day. A powerful mindset shift that centers your focus.

---

**🛡️ PRIVACY FIRST**

Your data never leaves your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. OrganizO stores everything locally — your data is yours alone.
